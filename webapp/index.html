How does it work?

Whale Party's dice game utilizes an oracle on the Ethereum blockchain (oraclize) to generate its randomness. It's provably fair and doesn't require signing up to play!

We also try to be as transparent as possible and your potential winning is calculated with oracle cost and our 1% fee taken out. Most dice game projects don't make the costs transparent and we have seen users (and have been victims of) "winning" when they are actually losing their hard earned ETH. (We are currently using an estimate of 0.0040 ETH for the oracle cost.)
